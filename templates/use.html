How to Use

1️⃣ Click Continue on the welcome screen.
2️⃣ Choose Encrypt or Decrypt on the next screen.
3️⃣ Upload or drag & drop your image file.
4️⃣ For decryption, upload both encrypted image and key file.
5️⃣ Click Start Encryption or Start Decryption.
6️⃣ Wait for the glowing loader to complete.
7️⃣ Download your encrypted/decrypted image instantly.
🔑 Always store the key file safely — without it, decryption is impossible ❗
